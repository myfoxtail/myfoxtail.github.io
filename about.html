Hi there

I am an introvert and a passionate thinker. I see the world as fascinating and believe design has an enormous power to make people more curious about it.

I dedicate myself to studying and practising graphic design along with my long-lasting career as a software engineer, applying my enthusiasm for complex design systems, design theory and problem-solving.

Design is something I wanted to do since early days of my professional career. Even though I chose to focus and become a software engineer I always had a chance to get involved in the visual design in my work. In 2023 I took it seriously and got some ground knowledge by studying at Shillington.

If I could live two more lives in parallel, I would choose to be a neuroscientist and a journalist. I got into neurosciences a long ago after reading The Humane Interface by Jef Raskin. As to journalism, I am more than fascinated by certain figures and teams in the world of journalism and the way they bring the truth out there and make the world better.

I have been also playing piano since I was 5 years old and writing songs as a way of expression. Productionasing, however, is not my thing but I'm trying to get it out there when I have time. You can check out this website. I produce under name Sunlyse.
