To make the world a curious place to be

SUNLIES is a small studio run from Sydney, Australia. It was started with a strong believe that design has an enormous power to make people more curious about the world.

SUNLIES is run by Alexandra Lui.

She is an introvert and a passionate thinker. Alex dedicates herself to studying and practising graphic design along with her long-lasting career as a software engineer, applying her enthusiasm for complex design systems, design theory and problem-solving.

Design is something she wanted to do since early days of her professional career. Even though she chose to focus and become a software engineer she always had a chance to get involved in the visual design at work.

If Alex could live two more lives in parallel, she would choose to be a neuroscientist and a journalist.

Alex has also been playing piano since she was 5 years old and writing songs as a way of expression. Production is quite slow but she is trying to get it out there when she has time. You can check out this website. She produces under the name Sunlyse.
